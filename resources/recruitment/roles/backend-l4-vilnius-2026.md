# 2 Level-4 (Lead) Back End Software Engineers in Vilnius

## Location and Working Pattern

* Based in Vilnius.
* Working fully remote for the foreseeable future.
  * Possibility of moving to a hybrid working pattern if we open an engineering office in Vilnius.

## Role and Level

* Back end software engineer
* Level 4 (Lead)
  * Doesn't imply that the candidate is the singular technical lead on the team
  * Still required to provide technical leadership, possibly on a project-by-project basis
  * Should be proactive and entrepreneurial - actively spotting opportunitites to improve our software, and advocating for them
  * Fully engaged in technical discussionsm able to for technical opinions and defend them
* Would consider candidates operating at a higher lvel, e.g. Staff Engineers, and could raise the role level accordingly

## Technical Skills

* Java is a hard requirement
* Cloud-native design
  * Ideally with AWS, but other cloud platforms are valid
* Engineering with generative AI
  * We want people who are actively engaging with AI in how they work and finding ways to boost their productivity

## Personal Skills

* Self-reliant and self disciplined
  * Will be working remotely, but as a member of a team that's largely co-located.  Will need to be able to cope with this.
* English Language
  * Able to work effectively in English

## Signals to Look For

* Evidence of actual leadership
  * CV draws attention to projects led, especially ones that are proactively initiated
  * Avoid CVs that stay in the "contributed to...", "worked on...", "delivered..." lane.  
* Evidence of engineering maturity and experience
* Good fit for role
  * commited back end engineer
  * Not related engineer looking to make a switch
